# Top 10 Apps You Must Have When Living in China

Moving to China for studies? Here are the essential apps you need to download before you arrive. These apps will help you navigate daily life, from paying for meals to ordering delivery.

## 1. WeChat (微信)

WeChat is the super-app that does everything:

- **Messaging** - Stay connected with friends and family
- **Payments** - WeChat Pay is accepted everywhere
- **Mini Programs** - Access services without downloading separate apps
- **Official Accounts** - Follow news and university updates

## 2. Alipay (支付宝)

The primary competitor to WeChat Pay, Alipay offers:

- Mobile payments at all merchants
- Utility bill payments
- Transportation cards
- International money transfers

## 3. Didi (滴滴出行)

China's answer to Uber:

- Book rides quickly
- Multiple car options
- English interface available
- Integration with Alipay and WeChat Pay

## 4. Meituan (美团)

Your all-in-one lifestyle app:

- Food delivery
- Movie tickets
- Hotel bookings
- Discounts at restaurants

## 5. Ele.me (饿了么)

Another major food delivery platform:

- Wide restaurant selection
- Frequent promotions
- Real-time order tracking

## 6. Amap (高德地图) / Baidu Maps

Essential for navigation:

- Detailed maps of China
- Public transit directions
- Real-time traffic updates
- Walking and driving directions

## 7. Pleco

The ultimate Chinese dictionary app:

- OCR camera translation
- Handwriting recognition
- Flashcard system
- Offline functionality

## 8. Ctrip (携程)

For travel within China:

- Train tickets
- Flight bookings
- Hotel reservations
- Tour packages

## 9. Taobao (淘宝)

China's largest e-commerce platform:

- Everything from textbooks to electronics
- Competitive prices
- Buyer protection

## 10. Ximalaya (喜马拉雅)

For audio content:

- Podcasts in Chinese
- Audiobooks
- Language learning content
- Entertainment

## Quick Setup Guide

```
1. Download apps before arriving in China
2. Set up WeChat with your phone number
3. Link a bank card (Chinese bank account recommended)
4. Enable payment features
5. Explore mini-programs within WeChat
```

## Pro Tips

- **VPN**: Have a reliable VPN ready for accessing international services
- **Chinese Phone Number**: Essential for most apps
- **Bank Account**: Open a Chinese bank account within your first week
